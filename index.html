About

I’m Zach — Tennessee-born, Brooklyn-based, and usually juggling a few too many hobbies at once. Music is my main thread (guitar, mandolin, banjo, dobro, fiddle…), but I also get a kick out of photography, biking, and leather work.

Over the years I’ve picked up a few unexpected titles: worship pastor in Montana, gymnastics coach in Arizona, cowboy reenactor in Texas — and now, somehow, a truss designer in Brooklyn. I try to keep life lighthearted, creative, and full of good stories.

Projects

  • The West Side Cowboys — Honky-tonk/bluegrass band keeping cowboy songs alive in NYC.
  • New York Bluegrass — A hub for festivals, jams, and roots music in the city.
